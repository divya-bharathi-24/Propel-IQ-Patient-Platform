# User Story - US_018

## Story ID

- ID: US_018

## Story Title

- Waitlist Enrollment and Preferred Slot Swap

## Description

- As a patient, I want to enroll in a waitlist for a preferred unavailable time slot while booking an available alternative, so that the system automatically swaps my appointment to my preferred slot when it becomes available without manual intervention.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** I am booking an appointment and my preferred slot is unavailable, **When** I select the preferred slot for waitlist enrollment, **Then** the system books me into the available alternative slot and enrolls me in the waitlist for the preferred slot with FIFO ordering via enrollment timestamp.

2. **Given** I am on the waitlist, **When** the preferred slot becomes available (via cancellation or reschedule), **Then** the system automatically cancels my original booking, books me into the preferred slot, and releases the original slot back to availability.

3. **Given** the preferred slot is released, **When** multiple patients are on the waitlist for the same slot, **Then** the system processes in FIFO order and only the first patient gets the swap.

4. **Given** a successful swap occurs, **When** the swap is completed, **Then** the system notifies me via both email and SMS with the updated appointment details.

5. **Given** the preferred slot is claimed by a direct booking before waitlist processing, **When** the system processes the waitlist, **Then** it retains my original booking, removes the waitlist entry, and notifies me that the preferred slot is no longer available.

6. **Given** I am on the waitlist, **When** the original slot is released after a successful swap, **Then** the released slot triggers its own waitlist check for other patients.

## Edge Cases

- What happens when a patient on the waitlist cancels their original appointment? (Waitlist entry is also removed; patient is no longer eligible for the swap)
- How does the system handle a swap when the patient's notification channels fail? (Swap completes regardless; notification retry logic applies; appointment details visible in patient dashboard)
