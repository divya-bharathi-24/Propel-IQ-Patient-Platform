# User Story - US_020

## Story ID

- ID Format: US_020

## Story Title

- Appointment Cancellation & Rescheduling

## Description

- As an authenticated Patient, I want to cancel or reschedule an existing upcoming appointment through self-service, so that my calendar is always accurate and the released slot becomes immediately available to other patients.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** I have a booked upcoming appointment, **When** I click "Cancel Appointment" and confirm, **Then** the Appointment status is updated to `Cancelled`, the slot is released to the availability pool immediately, and the Redis slot cache is invalidated.

2. **Given** I cancel an appointment, **When** the cancellation is confirmed, **Then** any scheduled future reminders for that appointment are suppressed, and any active CalendarSync event is deleted from my connected calendar.

3. **Given** I choose to reschedule, **When** I select a new available slot and confirm, **Then** the original Appointment record is cancelled, a new Appointment record is created for the new slot, and the PDF confirmation for the new booking is emailed within 60 seconds.

4. **Given** I have an active WaitlistEntry for a preferred slot, **When** I cancel my current appointment, **Then** the WaitlistEntry is also cancelled and the preferred slot reservation is released.

## Edge Cases

- What happens if I try to cancel an appointment in the past? (System rejects cancellation with a validation error: "Cannot cancel a past appointment")
- What happens if rescheduling fails because the new slot was taken between selection and confirmation? (HTTP 409 Conflict returned; I am redirected to the slot picker to choose again)
