# User Story - US_022

## Story ID

- ID Format: US_022

## Story Title

- Insurance Soft Pre-Check & Status Display

## Description

- As a Patient booking an appointment, I want the system to soft-check my insurance details against internal records and show me a clear status (Verified / Not Recognized / Incomplete), so that I am aware of my insurance standing without my booking being blocked.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** I enter my insurer name and member ID in the booking flow, **When** the insurance pre-check runs, **Then** the result is one of: "Verified" (match found in dummy insurer records), "Not Recognized" (no match), or "Incomplete" (missing insurer name or member ID).

2. **Given** the insurance check returns "Not Recognized", **When** the result is displayed, **Then** guidance text explains what steps to take and the booking flow proceeds to the confirmation step without blocking.

3. **Given** the insurance check returns "Verified" or any status, **When** the booking is confirmed, **Then** an InsuranceValidation record is stored with the provider name, insurance ID, result, and timestamp.

4. **Given** I choose to skip the insurance step entirely, **When** I proceed to confirmation, **Then** the booking completes with an InsuranceValidation record marked as `result = Incomplete` and the status displayed on my dashboard.

## Edge Cases

- What happens if the insurance pre-check service is unavailable during booking? (Booking proceeds; insurance status is set to "Check Pending" and displayed as informational only)
- What happens when I enter partial insurance information (name only, no member ID)? (System returns "Incomplete" status with a specific prompt to provide the missing field)
