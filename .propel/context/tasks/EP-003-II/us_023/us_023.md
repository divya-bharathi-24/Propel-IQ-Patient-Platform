# User Story - US_023

## Story ID

- ID Format: US_023

## Story Title

- Preferred Slot Designation & Waitlist FIFO Enrollment

## Description

- As a Patient booking an available slot, I want to simultaneously designate an unavailable slot as my preferred slot and be enrolled in a FIFO waitlist, so that I am automatically moved to my preferred slot when it becomes available.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** I am in the booking workflow and have selected an available slot, **When** I also designate an unavailable slot as preferred, **Then** a WaitlistEntry is created with my `patientId`, `currentAppointmentId`, `preferredDate`, `preferredTimeSlot`, `enrolledAt`, and `status = Active`.

2. **Given** multiple patients waitlist the same preferred slot, **When** the slot opens, **Then** the patient with the earliest `enrolledAt` timestamp is selected first (FIFO ordering).

3. **Given** I designate a preferred slot, **When** I view my dashboard, **Then** I see a "Preferred Slot Waitlisted" indicator for my appointment with the preferred date and time displayed.

4. **Given** I decide to cancel my waitlist preference, **When** I remove the preferred slot designation, **Then** my WaitlistEntry status is set to `Expired` and the preferred slot position is released for other patients.

## Edge Cases

- What happens if I try to designate a slot that is actually available at the moment of booking? (System prompts me to book that slot directly instead of adding it to the waitlist)
- What happens if I cancel my current appointment while on the waitlist? (WaitlistEntry is set to `Expired` and the preferred slot is released as covered in US_020)
