# User Story - US_019

## Story ID

- ID: US_019

## Story Title

- AI Conversational Patient Intake

## Description

- As a patient, I want to complete my intake through an AI-powered conversational interface that collects my demographics, medical history, symptoms, and medications via natural language interaction, so that I can provide my health information in a comfortable, guided conversation rather than filling out a long form.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** I have a booked appointment, **When** I initiate the intake process and select AI conversational mode, **Then** the AI engine presents the first intake question in natural language covering demographics.

2. **Given** the AI asks a question, **When** I respond in natural language, **Then** the AI interprets my response, extracts structured data (demographics, medical history, symptoms, medications), and presents the next relevant question.

3. **Given** I have answered all intake questions, **When** the conversation completes, **Then** the system displays a summary of all collected data organized by category for my review.

4. **Given** the AI cannot interpret my response, **When** confidence is low, **Then** the AI rephrases the question with clarifying options to help me provide the information.

5. **Given** I confirm the intake summary, **When** the data is saved, **Then** the system creates an IntakeRecord with source indicator "AI" linked to my patient profile and appointment.

6. **Given** the AI conversation is in progress, **When** I provide a response that updates a previously collected field, **Then** the system updates the specific field without restarting the intake from the beginning.

## Edge Cases

- What happens when the AI service is unavailable during intake? (System displays fallback message and offers to switch to manual form intake with any partially collected data preserved)
- How does the system handle multilingual patient responses? (Phase 1 supports English only; non-English responses trigger a clarification prompt suggesting manual form)
