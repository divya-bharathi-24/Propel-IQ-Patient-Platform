# User Story - US_031

## Story ID

- ID Format: US_031

## Story Title

- No-Show Risk Score Calculation & Color-Coded Staff Display

## Description

- As a Staff member, I want to see a calculated no-show risk score (Low / Medium / High) with color-coded severity indicators next to each appointment, so that I can proactively identify at-risk patients before they miss their appointment.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** a new appointment is booked, **When** the risk calculation job runs, **Then** a NoShowRisk record is created for the appointment with a score (0–1 decimal), a severity label (Low / Medium / High), a `factors` JSONB field listing contributing indicators (prior no-show history, booking lead time, appointment type, intake completion, reminder engagement), and a `calculatedAt` timestamp.

2. **Given** I view the Staff appointment management interface, **When** the appointment list loads, **Then** each appointment displays its risk label with a color-coded badge (green = Low, amber = Medium, red = High).

3. **Given** the AI augmentation service is unavailable, **When** the risk score is computed, **Then** the rule-based scoring engine runs independently and produces a score without AI augmentation, logging the degraded mode.

4. **Given** an appointment's risk factors change (e.g., intake is completed after the initial score), **When** the recalculation job runs, **Then** the NoShowRisk record is updated with the new score and a new `calculatedAt` timestamp.

## Edge Cases

- What happens if a patient has no prior appointment history for behavioral scoring? (Absence of history counts as neutral; the risk score defaults to Medium until behavioral data accumulates)
- What happens when a risk score cannot be calculated due to missing required data fields? (Score defaults to Medium; a `factors` entry is added noting "Insufficient data for full assessment")
