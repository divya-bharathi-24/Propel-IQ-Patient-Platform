# User Story - US_032

## Story ID

- ID Format: US_032

## Story Title

- High-Risk Appointment Flag with Recommended Interventions

## Description

- As a Staff member, I want appointments assessed as High-risk to be automatically flagged with a set of recommended intervention actions that I must explicitly accept or dismiss, so that no high-risk patient is overlooked before their appointment.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** an appointment risk score is classified as High, **When** the Staff appointment list loads, **Then** a prominent "High-Risk" flag banner is displayed on the appointment card with a list of recommended interventions (e.g., "Send additional reminder", "Request callback").

2. **Given** a High-risk flag is displayed, **When** I explicitly click "Accept" on a recommended intervention, **Then** the intervention is marked as accepted with my staff ID and timestamp, and the relevant action (e.g., ad-hoc reminder) is triggered.

3. **Given** a High-risk flag is displayed, **When** I click "Dismiss", **Then** the flag is acknowledged with my staff ID and a dismissal reason (optional), and the flag no longer appears as a pending action for that appointment.

4. **Given** a High-risk appointment flag is unacknowledged, **When** I view the Staff dashboard, **Then** unacknowledged High-risk flags are prominently surfaced in a "Requires Attention" section sorted by appointment time.

## Edge Cases

- What happens if a High-risk score drops to Medium before I acknowledge the flag? (The flag is automatically cleared; the action history retains the prior flag for audit purposes)
- What happens if I am not logged in when a High-risk flag is generated? (The flag remains in "Requires Attention" until any Staff member acknowledges it on next login)
