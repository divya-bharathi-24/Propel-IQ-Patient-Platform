# User Story - US_040

## Story ID

- ID: US_040

## Story Title

- No-Show Risk Score Calculation

## Description

- As a scheduling system, I want to calculate a no-show risk score for each appointment using rule-based factors augmented by AI pattern recognition from historical data, so that staff can identify high-risk patients and target them with proactive interventions.

## Acceptance Criteria

1. **Given** an appointment is booked, **When** the risk assessment engine runs, **Then** the system calculates a no-show risk score (decimal 0-1) using rule-based factors including appointment history, lead time, and reminder engagement.

2. **Given** the rule-based score is calculated, **When** historical appointment data is available, **Then** the AI augments the risk model by identifying patterns from past no-show behavior.

3. **Given** the risk score is calculated, **When** the result is stored, **Then** a NoShowRisk record is created with score, contributing factors (JSONB), and calculation timestamp linked to the appointment.

4. **Given** the risk calculation runs, **When** a patient has no appointment history, **Then** the system uses default risk factors (lead time, specialty averages) and assigns a moderate baseline score.

5. **Given** the risk calculation engine processes appointments, **When** it runs for a batch of appointments, **Then** each individual calculation completes within 5 seconds.

## Edge Cases

- What happens when the AI model is unavailable? (System falls back to rule-based scoring only; score is flagged as "Rule-based only" in factors)
- How does the system handle risk recalculation when appointment details change? (Recalculate on reschedule; previous NoShowRisk record is replaced with updated calculation)
